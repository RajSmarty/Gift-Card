about us

YouGotaGift.com is the leading Digital Gift Card Company in the Middle East.

Our digital gifting platform is used by individuals and businesses to celebrate, reward, motivate and show appreciation to friends, loved ones, employees, customers and business partners.

Our business solution has also been adopted by major customer loyalty programs across leading telcos, banks, airlines delivering unparalleled choice of rewards, a memorable customer service experience, and best-in-class technology tailored to partner needs.

FAQs

The Wedding Registry

Why use the Wedding Registry?

The Wedding Registry allows couples to create an online Wedding Registry that is unique & flexible. With this registry, you will truly avoid unwanted gift items in exchange for gift cards which you can use to purchase what you want.

How secure is the Wedding registry?

Security is our top priority. YouGotaGift.com uses a secure & verified payment gateway with Visa and MasterCard.

In which currency can I create my Wedding Registry?

You can create your Wedding Registry in either AED or USD.

How can I track my Wedding Registry?

You will receive an alert every time your guest contributes to your Wedding Registry and you can manage your contributions through your account

When should I open my Wedding Registry?

We advise couples to open their Wedding Registry at least three months in advance.

Do I need any special documents to close my Wedding Registry?

Yes, we ask for your Marriage Certificate and your Passport Copies, for security reasons, while closing your Wedding Registry.
